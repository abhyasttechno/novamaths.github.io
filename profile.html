Areas for Improvement

Algebra: Solving Linear Equations

Suggestion: Focus on exercises involving multi-step equations. Practice isolating variables and pay close attention to the order of operations. Using visual aids can help understand the balance on both sides of the equation.

Geometry: Properties of Circles

Suggestion: You seem to be struggling with theorems related to chords and tangents. Review the definitions and try to solve practice problems that specifically ask to find lengths or angles using these properties.

Fractions: Mixed Operations

Suggestion: Difficulty arises when adding and multiplying fractions in the same problem. Remember to find a common denominator for addition/subtraction *before* performing multiplication. Work through problems step-by-step.

Word Problems: Translation

Suggestion: The main challenge is converting word problems into mathematical expressions. Practice by highlighting key phrases and numbers, and identify the underlying mathematical operation being asked for (e.g., "total" implies addition, "left" implies subtraction).
